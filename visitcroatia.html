ABOUT CROATIA

Croatia (Croatian: Hrvatska, pronounced [xř̩ʋaːtskaː]), officially the Republic of Croatia (Croatian: Republika Hrvatska),
is a country in Southeast Europe.
It borders Slovenia to the northwest, Hungary to the northeast, Serbia to the east, Bosnia and Herzegovina,
and Montenegro to the southeast, sharing a maritime border with Italy. Its capital,
Zagreb, forms one of the country's primary subdivisions, along with twenty counties.
Croatia has an area of 56,594 square kilometres (21,851 square mile)
and a population of 4.07 million, most of whom are Roman Catholics.
